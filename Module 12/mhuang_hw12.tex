We are given the stochastic differential equation for the Ornstein-Uhlenbrek process:
\[
  dX(t) = \theta (\mu - X(t))dt + \sigma dW(t)
\]
with the analytical solution as 
\[
  X(t) = X(0)e^{-\theta t} + \mu(1 - e^{-\theta t}) + \int_{0}^{t} \sigma e^{\theta (s - t)} dW(s)
\]
